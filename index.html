Yes. Prices come from Twelve Data with real market data. The free plan includes data with a 15-minute delay, the Pro plan includes real-time data.

Yes, SmartStockSignal works perfectly on any device — phone, tablet or computer. No app download needed.
What stocks can I analyze? +
You can analyze any NYSE and NASDAQ stock — Apple, Tesla, NVIDIA, Amazon, and thousands more. Also indices like SPY and QQQ.

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your profile. No contracts or penalties.

The AI analyzes up to 11 indicators simultaneously — RSI, MACD, VWAP, Smart Money, Put/Call Ratio, chart patterns and more — and generates an integrated recommendation with suggested entry price and stop loss.

No. SmartStockSignal is an educational technical analysis tool. The signals are references based on technical indicators and do not constitute financial advice. Always do your own research before trading.

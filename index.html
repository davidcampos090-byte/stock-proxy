📚 Educational Platform

We teach you how the pros read the market — not what to buy.

SmartStockSignal was built with one goal: to make professional-grade technical analysis accessible to everyone. Whether you're just starting out or have been trading for years, understanding indicators like RSI, MACD, VWAP and Smart Money flow is what separates informed decisions from guesswork.

Our AI doesn't just give you a signal — it explains why. Every analysis includes the reasoning behind the score, which indicators are bullish or bearish, what pattern has been detected, and where institutional money appears to be flowing. This is how you develop real market intuition over time.

We use real market data from Twelve Data, AI analysis powered by Claude (Anthropic), and a weighted scoring system that gives more importance to the most reliable indicators — VWAP and Smart Money carry double weight compared to standard oscillators, because the data shows they're more predictive.

The platform includes a full Indicator Guide with visual charts showing exactly how each indicator works, a Chart Pattern library with 15 real trading patterns and professional explanations, a Signal Accuracy tracker so you can see how past signals performed, and a Stock Screener that scans entire sectors for opportunities simultaneously.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is this financial advice?+
No. SmartStockSignal is an educational technical analysis platform. All signals and AI commentary are for informational purposes only and do not constitute financial advice. Always consult a qualified financial advisor before investing.
Are the prices real-time?+
Yes. We use Twelve Data for real market data. The free plan includes 15-minute delayed intraday data. The Pro plan includes real-time data across all timeframes.
What stocks can I analyze?+
Any NYSE and NASDAQ listed stock — Apple, Tesla, NVIDIA, Amazon, and thousands more. Also major ETFs like SPY, QQQ, GLD and sector funds.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes, cancel your Pro subscription anytime from your account settings. No contracts, no penalties. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
How does the weighted score work?+
We assign different weights to each indicator based on reliability. VWAP and Smart Money carry 2x weight, chart patterns 1.5x, and standard oscillators 1x. This means the final signal reflects what institutional-grade analysis prioritizes.
Does it work on mobile?+
Yes, SmartStockSignal is fully responsive and works on any device. You can also install it as a PWA (Progressive Web App) on your iPhone or Android for a native app experience.
